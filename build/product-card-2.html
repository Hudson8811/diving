Primary Electronics

All KISS rebreathers require some form of monitoring electronics to display the PO2 readings from your oxygen sensors. KISS offers a variety of options for all types of divers and their needs and budgets.

The Shearwater Petrel has become the standard in rebreather electronics due to its proven design, simple and intuitive interface, and outstanding customer support. Also available is the Shearwater NERD - the ultimate in rebreather monitoring and safety, as well as the Divesoft Freedom.

The options are as follows:

Shearwater Petrel - Hardwired

The hardwired version of the Petrel is what the name implies. The connection from the rebreather to the Petrel dive computer is hardwired and hence (semi) permanent. While the cable can be removed with a wrench, it cannot be unplugged from the dive computer. Hardwired computers lack convenience, but offer an increase in reliability, as the failure point of the plug is eliminated.

Shearwater Petrel - 4-PIN Detachable

The 4-pin Petrel is detachable from the rebreather head by means of a 4-pin Subcon cable. This allows the computer to be disconnected for storage and travel purposes. The 4-pin plug is on the head side of the cable, and the computer itself has about 30" of cable that is hardwired to the computer.

Please note! There are 4-pin cables and 5-pin cables used on Shearwater devices and they are NOT COMPATIBLE! Not only are the plugs different, but the means of transmitting data is different. 4-pin cables use analog data transmission, while 5-pin cables use digital data packets. If you are considering buying a used Shearwater Petrel with a Subcon cable, be sure to check if it's 4 or 5 pin before buying.

Shearwater NERD - 4-PIN Detachable

The NERD has taken the CCR computer industry by storm. Essentially a Petrel display, right in your dive mask. The information you need is always in your field of view increasing diver awareness.

Please note! There are 4-pin cables and 5-pin cables used on Shearwater devices and they are NOT COMPATIBLE! Not only are the plugs different, but the means of transmitting data is different. 4-pin cables use analog data transmission, while 5-pin cables use digital data packets. If you are considering buying a used Shearwater NERD with a Subcon cable, be sure to check if it's 4 or 5 pin before buying.

Fischer Cable only - 28 inches

For those who already have a rebreather dive computer with a Fischer port, you may select a Fischer cable only. The Shearwater Petrel is the most popular dive computer of this nature, however other brands are also capable of working as well. Please contact your dive instructor, or KISS Rebreathers for questions on compatibility.

The 28 inch version of the Fischer cable is for those who wish to have a wrist mount display.
